# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal admin users (owner + staff) managing the Fourth Pyramid website CMS from a desktop browser. Primary task on any given screen: create/edit products, review quote requests, update page content. Not a daily consumer app — used in focused bursts during business hours.

## Product Purpose

Admin CMS for Fourth Pyramid Plastic Industries (الهرم الرابع للصناعات البلاستيكية), a Jordanian plastic packaging manufacturer based in Amman. The admin manages the public-facing corporate website: product catalog (IBC containers, drums, packaging), quote requests from B2B clients, page content, and image gallery. Success means the team can update the site without a developer.

## Brand Personality

Professional, reliable, regional. An established Jordanian industrial manufacturer — grounded, not trendy. The interface should feel like a well-made tool: confident in its clarity, not trying to impress. Arabic-first company, bilingual interface.

## Anti-references

- Dark hacker aesthetic (no neon on black, no terminal feel, no VS Code dark mode vibes)
- Generic Tailwind SaaS clones (white cards, default blue buttons, every admin looks the same)

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over decoration** — every element earns its place. The admin serves the operator, not the designer.
2. **Industrial confidence** — the UI should feel as solid and reliable as the products it manages.
3. **Regional identity** — bilingual EN/AR, RTL support, design that doesn't feel imported from a US SaaS template.
4. **Low cognitive load** — operators are managing real business data; reduce friction at every step.
5. **Calm authority** — not playful, not aggressive. Composed and in control.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Bilingual (EN/AR) with full RTL layout swap. Reduced motion respected. Touch targets ≥44px for tablet use.
